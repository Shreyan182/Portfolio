Epic Eats



Epic Eats is a cutting-edge food delivery platform built with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) that redefines convenience and quality in meal delivery. Designed to connect food lovers with an array of local restaurants, EpicEats offers a seamless ordering experience with real-time tracking, personalized recommendations, and secure payment options. The platform emphasizes speed and reliability, ensuring that users can enjoy exceptional meals delivered right to their doorstep. Whether it's a quick snack or a gourmet feast, EpicEats transforms dining into an epic adventure, all through an intuitive and user-friendly interface.






EV Xpresso


EV Xpresso is an innovative EV charging app designed to streamline the electric vehicle charging experience by integrating convenience, efficiency, and sustainability. The app offers advanced slot booking, real-time GPS navigation to the nearest available charging stations, and real-time vacancy detection, ensuring that users can always find and reserve a spot easily. By leveraging renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, EV Xpresso promotes environmental sustainability and reduces reliance on non-renewable energy. With its user-friendly interface and eco-friendly focus, EV Xpresso provides a seamless, reliable, and green solution for electric vehicle owners, encouraging the adoption of EVs and contributing to a cleaner future.
